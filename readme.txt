== Description ==

Introducing SharePreview, the essential WordPress plugin that simplifies social media optimization for your content. With just a single click, seamlessly preview your post as it would appear on various social media platforms and verify sharing links directly from your post editor.

Instant Post Preview:
Press the "Preview" button directly from your post editor to see how your content will look on social media platforms. Quickly assess the title, description, and image to ensure a compelling preview that captures your audience's attention.
